Managing Stress

Stress is a natural response to challenges. Here are tools to help you manage it effectively.

üéØ Understanding Stress

Learn about stress triggers, symptoms, and how it affects your body and mind.

Learn More

üèÉ Physical Activity

Exercise is a powerful stress reliever. Even a 10-minute walk can help.

  • Take a short walk outdoors
  • Try yoga or stretching
  • Dance to your favorite music

üò§ Breathing Exercises

Try the 4-7-8 technique: Breathe in for 4, hold for 7, exhale for 8.

üìù Journaling

Writing down your thoughts can help process stress and gain perspective.

  • Write for 5-10 minutes daily
  • List three things you're grateful for
  • Brain dump all your worries

Managing Anxiety

üìö Understanding Anxiety

üåü Grounding Techniques

Use the 5-4-3-2-1 method to ground yourself during anxious moments:

  • 5 things you can see
  • 4 things you can touch
  • 3 things you can hear
  • 2 things you can smell
  • 1 thing you can taste

üéß Apps for Anxiety

Helpful apps for managing anxiety:

  • Headspace - Meditation & sleep
  • Calm - Anxiety relief
  • Rootd - Panic attack support
  • Sanvello - Mood tracking

üí≠ Cognitive Techniques

Challenge anxious thoughts by asking:

  • Is this thought realistic?
  • What evidence supports it?
  • What would I tell a friend?
  • What's the worst that could happen?

Mindfulness helps you stay present and connected to the moment, reducing stress and improving well-being.

üßò What is Mindfulness?

üå∏ Simple Practices
