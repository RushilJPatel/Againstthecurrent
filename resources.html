🌊 Anxiety Support

Resources for managing anxiety and worry

Understanding Anxiety

Learn about anxiety disorders and how they affect teens.

  • NIMH: Anxiety Disorders
  • ADAA: Anxiety Information
  • Anxiety Canada: Youth Resources

Coping Techniques

  • Grounding exercises (5-4-3-2-1 technique)
  • Progressive muscle relaxation
  • Cognitive reframing
  • Exposure therapy basics
  • Breathing techniques

When to Seek Help

Anxiety becomes a concern when it:

  • Interferes with daily activities
  • Causes physical symptoms
  • Lasts for weeks or months
  • Leads to avoidance behaviors

Talk to a counselor or healthcare provider if anxiety is impacting your life.

💙 Depression Support

Understanding depression and finding hope

Understanding Depression

Depression is more than feeling sad—it's a medical condition that requires support.

  • NIMH: Depression Information
  • SAMHSA: Depression Resources
  • JED Foundation: Depression in Teens

Warning Signs

Be aware of these symptoms:

  • Persistent sadness or hopelessness
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Changes in sleep or appetite
  • Fatigue or low energy
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Thoughts of self-harm

If you or someone you know is showing these signs, reach out for help immediately.

Getting Support

  • Talk to a trusted adult (parent, teacher, counselor)
  • See a mental health professional
  • Contact a crisis helpline (988)
  • Build a support network
  • Practice self-care

🧘 Mindfulness & Wellness

Tools for mental wellness and self-care

What is Mindfulness?

Mindfulness is the practice of being present in the moment without judgment.

  • Mindful: Getting Started
  • Greater Good: Mindful Breathing

Mindfulness Exercises

  • Body Scan: Progressive awareness of body sensations
  • Mindful Breathing: Focus on breath for 5-10 minutes
  • Walking Meditation: Mindful movement
  • Gratitude Practice: Daily reflection on positives

Self-Care Tips

  • Maintain regular sleep schedule
  • Eat nutritious meals
  • Stay physically active
  • Connect with friends
  • Set boundaries
  • Do activities you enjoy
