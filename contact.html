Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free?

Yes! All resources and information on Against the Current are completely free and accessible to everyone.

Do you provide therapy or counseling?

No, we're an educational and resource platform. We connect people to professional services but don't provide direct therapy.

Can I volunteer or contribute?

We'd love to hear from you! Use the contact form above and select "Volunteer Interest" to learn about opportunities.

How can I suggest a resource?

Please use the contact form and select "Resource Suggestion." We review all submissions carefully.

Is my information kept private?

Absolutely. We respect your privacy and never share your contact information with third parties.

What if I need immediate help?

Call 988 (National Suicide Prevention Lifeline) or 911 immediately. Our contact form is not monitored 24/7.
